Egypt

Egypt is one of the most famous travel destinations in the world because of its ancient history and culture. Visitors can see the Pyramids of Giza, the Egyptian Museum, the Nile River, and historic mosques and markets in Cairo.

Things to Do

Visit the pyramids, take a Nile cruise, explore Cairo, and learn about ancient Egyptian history.

Why Visit?

This destination was included because it gives travelers a mix of history, culture, food, and memorable experiences.
